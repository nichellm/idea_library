Biography

Nichelle Nicole. M. started her career working in the Department of Agriculture as a Pathways intern having graduated from the University of Maryland, Digital Cultures and Creativity Honors College. She has worked in various capacities in over 9 years of public service---serving as an Executive Assistant to leadership; an editor, publishing Directives policies to 30,000 federal subscribers nationwide; events coordinator and training instructor; a business intelligence analyst, curating reports for program optimization; and also a web designer.

This homesite was personally stylized and hand coded in HTML and CSS to best exhibit some of the skills and expertise gleaned from having such a rich and diverse background. Thank you for visiting, and we hope you enjoy viewing the merger of creative and technical talents wrought with time, determination, and a dedication to providing value. Also some of the main visuals were photographed right here in our Nations Capitol, with the main homepage photo being of Silver Spring, Maryland's horizon on an early dewy morning.
